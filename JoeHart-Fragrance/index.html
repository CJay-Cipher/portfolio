Project Inspiration

The inspiration for JoeHart Empire Fragrance came from my passion for fragrances and the joy they bring to people's lives. As a front-end developer, I wanted to create a platform where customers could easily discover and purchase their favorite scents. This project started as a personal venture during my studies at ALX and has evolved into a comprehensive portfolio project showcasing my skills in e-commerce development.
